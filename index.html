Hey Friends!

Grey Mountain Gaming is an American Narrative Wargames Club, inspired by European and British clubs of the same style, the primary purpose of the club is to provide rich and involved long-format narrative games for members and potential members by providing a wealth of miniatures, terrain, and play space directed by experienced games masters and writers in a series of consistently updated settings. This Patreon is intended to act as a fund to support the club's activities, the money earned here is intended for use on refreshments for gamedays, events venue costs, and the purchase of terrain and club armies and models for use in narrative scenarios determined by club leadership.

American Narrative Tabletop Wargames Club

Grey Mountain Gaming is a space for people to enjoy tabletop games, art, videogames, and other social activities! We enjoy meeting on and offline in developing a community centered around positivity and acceptance of people from different backgrounds out of desire to build a community. We host all major tabletop game systems like Warhammer 40,000, Horus Heresy, Age of Sigmar, Bolt Action, and others!
